Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our affiliate marketing platform.

How do I get started as a publisher?

Simply click on "Join as Publisher" button, fill out the registration form with your details, and our team will review your application within 24 hours. Once approved, you can start promoting offers immediately.

What are the payment terms?

We offer weekly and bi-weekly payment schedules with a minimum payout of $100. Payments are made via PayPal, bank transfer, or other preferred methods based on your location.

Do you provide marketing materials?

Yes! We provide banners, landing pages, email templates, and other creative materials for all our offers. Our creative team regularly updates materials to ensure optimal conversion rates.

How do you ensure traffic quality?

We use advanced fraud detection systems, real-time monitoring, and manual review processes to ensure all traffic is legitimate and high-quality. This protects both our advertisers and publishers.

Can I track my performance in real-time?

Absolutely! Our dashboard provides real-time statistics including clicks, conversions, earnings, and detailed analytics to help you optimize your campaigns for better performance.
